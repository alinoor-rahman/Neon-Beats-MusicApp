It’s a simple musical desktop application to compose music.
 Where there is a guitar, drums, 
 keyboard, musicpad and a karaoke platform. 
User can play a song through this application and at the same time can compose with guitar or keyboard or drums . 
Some sample chords are provided to help the user to compose. 
You can also sing a song in Karaoke Mode but to do that you have to choose a sound(karaoke video)
from your computer.
You can modify this desktop application if you want to.You can add the recording option.
